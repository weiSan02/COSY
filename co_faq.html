How may we help you?

Can I return an item?

Yes! We accept returns for any reason within 30 days of your order. Just send us an email with your Order Number and we will send you a return label.

When will I receive my item?

5-7 business days. We use Pos Laju shipping and pick your order by hand to make sure that everything is correct. If you need the delivery to be faster, do drop us an email!

Can I appoint the location to pickup my item?

Yes! We provide three delivery options. One of them is to appoint the location to pick up item. You may choose your option when doing payment.

What is your standard clothing size measurement?

It is UK based.

Can I pay with e-Wallet?

Yes! We offer two methods of payment, which are e-Wallet and cash.

Where can I check out my promo voucher?

You may check it out in your profile by clicking the "Profile" icon after you sign in your account.

How to apply for membership?

You may refer to the membership page.

Is it necessary to sign up an account before I purchase your products?

No. If you do not have an account on our website, you are required to enter your address and contact information for each of your purchase.
