About Us

Our Value

Cosy believe fashion can improve your beauty and self-confidence.
We serve our customers at a fair price and good quality products. We are targeting the market which is composed of men, women, teenagers and even kids in a wide range of ages. We hope to serve all the possible network users from different countries regardless of their gender, age and races who seek fashion forward pieces to enhance their wardrobe.
Thus, our customer will be able to express their own personal fashion style and even explore a brand new style which is unique and irreplaceable.

History

Our brand, Cosy was established on 13th July 2020 and owned by Joey, Diong, Lee, Eun and Joel Ling. We started our business because fashion is our interest and we can also develop our own fashion idea and creativity. Hence, we are looking forward to designing more products in the future.
