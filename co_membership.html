Membership

Do you want to become one of us? Let's join and become one of the members!

How to apply for membership?

  1. Click here
  2. Fill in your name, age, birthday date, IC number, email address, contact number, gender
  3. Choose the membership type (Adults/Teenagers/Kids).
  4. Upload one copy of your IC card as the supporting documentation.
  5. Click 'Submit'.
  6. You will receive a verification email that contains your member ID and one verification number.

Point Collecting System

Point Collecting System was created to encourage our customers to apply for membership and purchase our products. In other words, this system is only available for our members. Through purchasing our products up to certain prices, you will obtain points. For example, 1 point is rewarded for each RM10 of purchase. Once you reach 100 points, you will get the opportunity to join our lucky draw. The top three prizes in the lucky draw, which will be our best-selling products, alter every month. In addition, once you successfully collect 1000 points, you will receive a mysterious grand prize. In order to get the prize, you are to send us an email. You must state the following information in your email:

  1. Name (same as IC)
  2. Member ID
  3. Verification number
